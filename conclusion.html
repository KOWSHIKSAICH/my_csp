In conclusion, most of the people in the Usmansahabpet are preferring to use Airtel, Jio. Because of high-speed data strength and wide accessibility. These two companies provide best service to its subscribers. Some people are also using VI, BSNL also. But the service of Airtel and Jio is excellent and good respectively in the Area.
